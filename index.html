Biography

I am currently a faculty member in the Department of Computer Science and Engineering at Sri Sivasubramaniya Nadar College of Engineering. My research interests include reliable and approximate adder and multiplier designs, as well as Machine Learning/Deep Learning accelerators using reconfigurable computing. I am also keen on designing customized hardware solutions using reconfigurable computing for specific applications.

I received my Ph.D. from the Indian Institute of Information Technology, Design and Manufacturing (IIITDM), Kancheepuram, Chennai, where I was supervised by Dr. Noor Mahammad Sk (Associate Professor, IIITDM Kancheepuram)
